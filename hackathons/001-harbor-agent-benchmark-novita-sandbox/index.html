Hackathon Rules

Simple rules keep the public leaderboard comparable, reproducible, and fair.

Multiple entries allowed Each participant can submit multiple works across tracks. Only the highest eligible score per track is considered.
One prize per participant If one participant wins multiple tracks, they receive one award; other track awards roll down to the next eligible participant.
Discord record required Every submitted work must have a matching submission record in the relevant Discord channel.
Real Sandbox run required A genuine Novita Sandbox run report is required for eligibility, along with a public Harbor Hub job link.
No fake or copied entries False demos, non-real runs, plagiarism, or artificial traffic manipulation will result in disqualification.
Community incentives Selected high-quality run reports, technical analyses, bug/risk cases, and helpful community support may receive $20-$50 credits.

Rewards

Credits reward strong benchmark performance and useful community contributions.

$100 trial credits All individual users can receive $100 Novita free trial credits after registering.
$300 per track winner Each track winner receives $300 Novita credits, subject to the one-prize-per-participant rule.
$20-$50 community credits Small credit awards may be used for high-quality reports, useful analysis, bug/risk cases, and participant support.

Startup Program

Startup Program is not required for the Challenge. AI startups with a company entity can apply directly for up to $10,000 credits, especially teams working on Coding Agents, Evals, DevTools, AI Infra, Sandbox, or unknown-code execution.

After the event, Novita may follow up with teams that have a company entity, real commercial scenarios, strong project quality, clear production Sandbox needs, or partnership and credits interest.
